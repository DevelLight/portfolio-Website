How does the Vegan and Veggie Check work?

A barcode contains data that is read by the camera. This data is then sent to an open source food database in which the barcode content is searched for. If the product is found, there is a response with additional information about it. It also indicates whether the product is vegan or vegetarian. This information is then displayed to the user on the website.
